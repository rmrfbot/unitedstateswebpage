Metro Areas: American cities often sprawl across independently managed suburbs. For instance, Dallas (with a city population of 1.2 million) is part of the Dallas-Fort Worth-Arlington metro area, which boasts 7.9 million people. Similar patterns exist for cities like New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago.
